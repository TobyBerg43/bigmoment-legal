Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 6, 2026 · Applies to the BigMoment iOS app

Who we are

BigMoment ("we", "us") is an iOS app that turns your milestone details and photos into AI-generated shareable graphics.

What we collect

Account data: when you sign in with Apple we receive an account identifier and, if you share it, your email address (managed by Supabase, our authentication provider). Content you provide: the details you type (names, schools, dates, messages) and the photos you upload, which are required to create your graphic. Purchase data: payments are processed entirely by Apple through In-App Purchase; we never see your payment details. We store a record of verified purchases and your generation balance. Technical data: standard server logs used for security and rate limiting.

How your photos and details are used

Uploaded photos and the details you enter are sent to OpenAI's image generation API solely to create your graphic. Photos and generated graphics are stored privately (Supabase) so you can access your past creations. We do not sell your photos, use them for advertising, or use them to train our own models.

Children

The app is intended for adults (parents, family members, agents). Graphics often depict minors (e.g. student athletes); by uploading a photo you confirm you have the right and any necessary consent to use it. Accounts may not be created by children under 13.

Third parties we rely on

Apple (sign-in and payments), Supabase (authentication, database, file storage), and OpenAI (image generation). Each processes data only as needed to provide the service.

Retention & deletion

We keep your photos and graphics while your account exists so you can re-save them. You can request deletion of your account and all associated photos, graphics, and personal data at any time by emailing us; we will complete deletion within 30 days, except records we must keep for legal or accounting reasons.

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data. Contact us and we'll honor applicable requests.

Security

Photos and graphics live in private storage accessible only via short-lived signed URLs. API keys are held server-side only.

Changes & contact

We'll post any changes on this page. Questions or requests: support@bigmoment.app.
